Enhancing Meditron Accuracy With Meditree

Methods


Developed Meditree, a Tree of thought inference method that led to 5% improvement on medical benchmarks for our Medical LLM.

Results


Meditron outperforms its base model by 3% on medical benchmarks, and our Meditree inference method allows us to reach GPT4-Base performance of 80% accuracy on medical benchmarks, a 5% gain over Meditron alone.
